You must not use, attempt to use, or help another person use the Service to:

1. **Break the law.** Commit, facilitate, or attempt any act that violates

2. **Infringe others' rights.** Infringe, misappropriate, or violate any
   intellectual property, publicity, privacy, contractual, fiduciary,
   confidentiality, or other right of any third party.

3. **Record without consent.** Record, transcribe, transmit, or process
   the voice, communications, or personal information of any person in a
   manner not permitted by law, including (without limitation) any
   all-party-consent recording law. You are responsible for securing
   consents before enabling dictation.

4. **Violate privilege or professional obligations.** Send material
   subject to legal professional privilege, medical confidentiality,
   accounting confidentiality, banking secrecy, or any analogous
   obligation to any third-party provider unless you are authorised to do
   so and have satisfied yourself that the provider's handling is
   consistent with that obligation.

5. **Target children or vulnerable people.** Use the Service to generate,
   solicit, or distribute material that sexualises, exploits, endangers,
   or is otherwise harmful to children or to vulnerable persons.

6. **Hate, harassment, and threats.** Produce, distribute, or facilitate
   content that incites violence, harasses, stalks, threatens, doxxes, or
   discriminates against any person or group on the basis of a protected

7. **Fraud, deception, and impersonation.** Use the Service to commit
   fraud, phishing, social engineering, market manipulation, fake
   reviews, deceptive legal or accounting practices, or to impersonate

8. **Sensitive-use abuse.** Use the Service to make decisions about credit,
   employment, housing, insurance, immigration, or access to education,

   applicable legal standard.

9. **Weapons, malware, and critical systems.** Use the Service in
   connection with weapons systems; the development or operation of

10. **Abuse the platform.** Interfere with, disrupt, or place an undue
    load on the Service or the third-party services it calls; reverse

11. **Violate Third-Party Service terms.** Use the Service in a way that
    violates Deepgram's, OpenAI's, Anthropic's, or any other integrated
    provider's terms of service, usage policy, or acceptable-use policy.

12. **Infect others.** Upload, input, or transmit any virus, worm, Trojan,
