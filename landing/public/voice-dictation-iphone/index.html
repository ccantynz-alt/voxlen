Frequently Asked Questions

How does Voxlen voice dictation work on iPhone?

The Voxlen keyboard for iPhone and iPad is coming soon. Join the waitlist to be notified when it ships.

Does Voxlen work in every iPhone app?

Yes. Because Voxlen is a system-level keyboard extension, it works in any app that accepts text input — Mail, Notes, Safari, Word, Outlook, LexisNexis, Clio, iManage, and thousands more. No copy-pasting required — text is typed directly where your cursor is.

Is there an offline voice dictation mode for iPhone?

The mobile keyboard is on the waitlist. Fully offline Privileged Mode is available now in the desktop app, using on-device Whisper and on-device grammar so nothing leaves the machine.

Can I use iPhone voice dictation for legal documents?

Yes. Voxlen for iPhone includes the same legal clause library as the desktop app — say "insert indemnity clause" and it appears. It also recognises 26 Latin legal phrases, formats legal citations, and applies legal-specific grammar rules automatically.

Does Voxlen sync between iPhone and Mac?

Yes. Your Voxlen account syncs custom vocabulary, clause library, and settings across supported desktop devices; mobile sync details will be announced when the keyboards ship. Dictation history is stored locally on each device for privacy.
