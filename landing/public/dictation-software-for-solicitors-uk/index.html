FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from UK solicitors and barristers.

Is Voxlen AI compliant with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018?
Yes. Voxlen AI is fully compliant with UK GDPR (as retained in UK law post-Brexit under the European Union (Withdrawal) Act 2018) and the Data Protection Act 2018. Our local processing mode means all transcription occurs entirely on your device — no audio, no transcript data, no personal information is transmitted to or stored by Voxlen. This satisfies the data minimisation and storage limitation principles under UK GDPR Articles 5(1)(c) and 5(1)(e).
Is Voxlen AI suitable for SRA-regulated solicitors?
Yes. Voxlen AI's architecture is designed to support SRA Code of Conduct obligations around client confidentiality (paragraph 6.3). Because no client data leaves your device in local processing mode, there is no third-party data processor relationship to declare, no mandatory DPIA under Article 35 UK GDPR for routine dictation, and no risk of inadvertent disclosure. Your firm's COLP and COFA can review our data flow documentation on request.
Does Voxlen understand UK legal terminology — Crown Court, High Court, and so on?
Voxlen is pre-trained on UK legal vocabulary covering Crown Court, High Court of Justice (King's Bench Division, Chancery Division, Family Division), Court of Appeal, and the Supreme Court of the United Kingdom. It understands CPR procedure and practice directions, solicitor-specific terminology, the distinction between solicitors and barristers, and specialist vocabulary for conveyancing, corporate, employment, and family law. The on-device flywheel learns additional vocabulary specific to your practice area over time.
How accurate is Voxlen AI for legal dictation?
Voxlen achieves over 98% word-level accuracy on legal content using Deepgram Nova-2 for streaming transcription. AI grammar correction via Claude or GPT-4o-mini produces near publication-ready output — handling complex legal sentence structures, Latin phrases, and statutory references accurately. Accuracy improves further as the system learns your voice, vocabulary, and drafting patterns over time.
What platforms does Voxlen AI support?
Voxlen AI is available as a desktop app for macOS and Windows, and as an iOS keyboard extension for iPhone and iPad. Text is injected directly into any application — Word, case management systems (Leap, Clio, LEAP, Actionstep), Outlook, or any other application — via native OS keyboard simulation. No clipboard or copy-pasting required. Android support is on the roadmap.
