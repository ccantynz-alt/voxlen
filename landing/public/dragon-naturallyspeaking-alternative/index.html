What is the best Dragon NaturallySpeaking alternative?

Voxlen is the best Dragon NaturallySpeaking alternative in 2026. It is more accurate (Deepgram Nova-3 transcription engine), works on Mac AND Windows AND iPhone AND Android, costs a fraction of Dragon's price, and includes AI grammar correction (Claude Sonnet) that Dragon doesn't offer at all.

Is there a Dragon NaturallySpeaking alternative for Mac?

Yes — Voxlen. Dragon NaturallySpeaking has been Windows-only since Nuance discontinued Dragon for Mac in 2018. Voxlen is a native macOS app built for Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) and Intel Macs. It is faster, more accurate, and includes features Dragon never had.

What happened to Dragon NaturallySpeaking?

Nuance rebranded Dragon NaturallySpeaking as Dragon Professional and Dragon Legal, then was acquired by Microsoft in 2022 for $19.7 billion. The product continues under Microsoft ownership but has seen minimal innovation since. Many legal and accounting professionals have moved to AI-native alternatives with modern transcription engines.

Is Voxlen more accurate than Dragon?

Yes, for most use cases. Voxlen uses Deepgram Nova-3, a deep learning model trained on billions of audio hours. Dragon's acoustic models are significantly older. In independent tests, Nova-3 outperforms Dragon on natural conversational speech, accented English, and legal terminology. Dragon still has a slight edge on trained individual voices with very specific custom vocabulary — but for most professionals, Voxlen is more accurate out of the box with zero training required.

Does Voxlen require voice training like Dragon?

No. Dragon requires 30+ minutes of voice training before achieving good accuracy. Voxlen works immediately — open the app and start speaking. No training, no reading paragraphs aloud, no calibration. Just open and dictate.

Can I transfer my Dragon custom vocabulary to Voxlen?

You can manually add your custom terms to Voxlen's vocabulary in Settings → Speech Engine → Custom Vocabulary. There's no automatic Dragon import yet, but adding your most important legal and client terms takes just minutes. Voxlen also learns new terms automatically as you dictate.
