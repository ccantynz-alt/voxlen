How It Works on Android

Set up in under two minutes.

1

Install from Play Store

Download Voxlen from the Google Play Store. The app installs a custom keyboard (IME) that you enable in Android settings.

2

Set as Your Keyboard

Go to Settings → General Management → Language and Input → On-screen Keyboard. Enable Voxlen and optionally set it as your default.

3

Dictate Anywhere

Tap the microphone in any app. Speak naturally — Voxlen transcribes, AI-corrects, and types the text directly at your cursor position.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Voxlen voice dictation work on Android?

Voxlen installs as an Android IME (Input Method Editor) — the same mechanism as Gboard and SwiftKey. Once enabled in Android Settings, the Voxlen keyboard appears in every app. Tap the microphone to dictate; AI transcribes and polishes the text before typing it at your cursor.

Does Voxlen work on Samsung Galaxy phones?

Yes. Voxlen is optimised for Samsung Galaxy S and A series phones running Android 10 and above. It integrates with Samsung's keyboard settings and works in all Samsung and third-party apps installed from the Play Store.

Can I use voice dictation for legal documents on Android?

Yes. Voxlen for Android includes the full legal clause library — say "insert governing law clause" and the complete clause appears. It recognises Latin legal phrases, formats citations, and applies legal grammar rules from your Android phone.

Is Voxlen for Android free?

Yes, the free tier includes 50 minutes of transcription per month with basic grammar correction. Pro ($29/month) unlocks unlimited transcription, the full legal clause library, all languages, and advanced AI grammar correction.

Does Android voice dictation work offline?

Offline dictation is coming soon. Voxlen's Privileged Mode (coming soon, on the Professional plan) will use on-device Whisper transcription so no audio leaves your device — critical for lawyers and accountants handling sensitive client information on their Android phone. Today, Voxlen never stores your audio or transcripts — requests pass through zero-retention endpoints straight to the AI providers, and nothing is retained.
